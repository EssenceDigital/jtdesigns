My name is Jeanine. I’m an Interior Design Technologist.

My design philosophy comes from mixing the old and new—timelessness. We all learn from the past and are always learning from new experiences. I love reaching into the past, bringing back what is good, what is beautiful, and most of all, what is useful and can stand the test of time. I love to show the light, the dark, and the whimsicality of the space. The drama of life.
